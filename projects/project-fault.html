Emergency Fault Finding & Repair

Rapid diagnosis and safe fix of electrical faults

Project Overview

A client had intermittent power loss in half of the house. Using thermal imaging and multimeter tests, we traced the fault to a corroded junction box and loose neutral connection. Replaced damaged section and restored full power.

Key Highlights

  • Same‑day emergency response (2 hours)
  • Advanced diagnostic tools (thermal camera, circuit tracer)
  • Permanent solution with upgraded waterproof box
